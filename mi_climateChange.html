🌸 Ways to Help 🌸

Remember, every action counts! It may seem useless at the moment, but many simple actions over time will create a huge impact 😁

Ways YOU Can Make A Change:

Conserve Energy✨:

1. Switch to LED Lightbulbs 💡
2. Do laundry in Cold Water 💦
3. Get a Home Energy Audit
4. Remember to always turn off the lights when leaving a room
5. Unplug Unused Electronics
6. Use an Automatic Thermostat

Save Through FOOD🤩:

1. Eat Less Meat
2. Buy Local (ALWAYS SUPPORT LOCAL BUSINESSES)
3. Create a Community Garden 🌾
4. Reduce Your Food Waste
5. Switch to a Milk Alternative (such as oat or almond)
6. Planting your Own Garden
7. Composting!

Save Through Transportation!

1. Walk instead of Driving
2. Or Ride your Bike!
3. Utilize Pulic Transportation (Like the bus!🚎)
4. Pay Attention to your Carbon Footprint
5. Ensure your Gas Cap is Secure
6. Wash your Car in a Self-Serve Carwash
7. Fly Less
8. Turn off Your Engine: Don't Idle!
9. Utilize Ride Sharing Programs

Other Ways to Help the Planet:

1. Use Reusable Bags
2. Use Reusable Utensils
3. Use Reusable Straws
4. In General, Reduce the Amount of Single-Use Plastic you Use
5. Plant a Tree
6. Shop Seconhand
7. Inform Yourself through Books, Documentaries, and Podcasts
8. Vote for Climate Change

Last but not least...SPEAK UP! This is the best and most effective way to spread awareness! Awareness is the first step in getting more people in on this fight against climate change.

If you have any questions, reach us through the email listed in the "Contact Us" tab! Thank you!
