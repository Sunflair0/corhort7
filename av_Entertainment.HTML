Entertainment

What I enjoy to watch

Anime

I love to watch anime when ever I have free time. I started liking anime at the age of 14. It brings a vibrant sense of energy that animated shows and films impact. I like it also because, it offers a wide range of perspectives on life, culture, and everything else in between. It installs hope, every anime has a character dream they wish to achieve and they strive for it. It also brings a feeling of freedom.

Marvel

I'm more of a Marvel fan than a DC. Marvel just brings a unique ability to unite. No matter who you are or where you are from, for every person in the world there is a character they can aspire to. Also because, of the character developing and the ability to make the audience connect with them.

Crime / Procedural Drama

I always had an attraction to crime and procedural dramas. My first show I watched of this genre was Criminal Minds. And then the next show I found myself interested was 9-1-1. I love crime dramas because, they excel at weaving suspense-laden storylines that keep viewers on the edge of their seats. And I like procedural dramas because, the lack of long-term storylines makes it easier for viewers to tune in for just one episode without feeling lost.
