Book The Pioneers


The Pioneers by David McCullough is a book that traces the development of the first Ohio settlement beginning in the 1780s through to the mid-nineteenth century1. McCullough argues that the founding settlers of the Northwest Territory were motivated to spread the American ideals of religious freedom, opposition to slavery, and advancement of education westward.

The Piano Lesson


The Piano Lesson is a Pulitzer Prize-winning play written by August Wilson1. It is set in Pittsburgh in 1936 after the Great Depression234. The play tells the story of the Charles family and their plans for a family piano. The piano is decorated with designs carved by an enslaved ancestor.

The Wimpy Kid


Diary of a Wimpy Kid is an American children's book series and media franchise created by American author and cartoonist Jeff Kinney. The series follows Greg Heffley, a middle-schooler who illustrates his daily life in a diary (although he insists that it is a journal).
