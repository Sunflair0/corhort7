Book The Pioneers


The Pioneers by David McCullough is a book that traces the development of the first Ohio settlement beginning in the 1780s through to the mid-nineteenth century1. McCullough argues that the founding settlers of the Northwest Territory were motivated to spread the American ideals of religious freedom, opposition to slavery, and advancement of education westward.
