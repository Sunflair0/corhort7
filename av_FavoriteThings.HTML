My Favorite Things

Here are a few things I really love

Books

I started developing a love for reading books in my 7th grade year. Which is surprising because, at a very young age I never did like reading. I especially took a liking to Manga books. They brought me entertainment to me at my young age. I also took a liking to Mystery, Crime, and Romance books.

Coffee

Coffee is my favorite drink out everything there is to drink. I've become obsessed with drinking coffee I drink it every morning, when I come back from school and every night. I prefer homemade coffee instead of buying it from a cafe.

Drawing

I enjoy drawing when I have free time. I always been a creative girl at a young age. I enjoy drawing more anime, cartoons and characters from shows. I've never been a big fan of drawing more realistic pictures. I also prefer more my drawings to be black and white. Mainly because, I was never good at blending and matching colors well.
