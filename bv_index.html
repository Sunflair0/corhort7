Celtics History

The Boston Celtics are a professional basketball team based in Boston, Massachusetts. They were founded in 1946 and are one of the most successful franchises in NBA history.

The team has a rich history of success, including numerous NBA championships. Some of the greatest basketball players in history have played for the Celtics, contributing to their legacy as one of the most iconic teams in the league.

Throughout the years, the Celtics have built a tradition of excellence, teamwork, and dedication. They have had legendary rivalries with other teams, making their games highly anticipated and memorable for basketball fans worldwide.

Explore more about the Celtics' journey by attending games, following their progress, and celebrating the team's achievements with fellow fans.
